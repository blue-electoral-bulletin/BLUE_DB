Estonian results use three special units to report voters outside of their
district of residence (\texttt{EE\_EEYY97}, 2023), invalid votes only available
as a national aggregate (\texttt{EE\_EEYY98}, 2004, 2009), and e-voting that cannot
be attributed to a municipality (\texttt{EE\_EEYY99}, 2009, 2011).

In elections until 2014, registered voters, turnout, and invalid votes are only reported
at the county (\emph{maakond}) level. Counties are registered as special constituency-style units.
