French national election results are provided for the two rounds of the
\textit{Assemblée nationale} elections separately (round~1 and round~2).
Results are at the commune level (\textit{commune}).

Overseas collectivities and departments are included in the \texttt{FR} files
using the standard INSEE commune coding.  Additionally, the \texttt{FRZ}
hierarchy in \texttt{geo/special.csv} maps the collectivities to their
NUTS-equivalent codes for aggregate analysis.
Special units for aggregate counts at the level of full collectivities
(\texttt{FR\_97701}, \texttt{FR\_98801}...) are used in early elections.
